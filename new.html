Frequent Questions


Where are drones allowed to fly?


Generally, we prefer to route flights away from populated areas. our aircraft have been certified for long distance operations beyond visual line of sight. The details depend on your local aviation regulations.

What’s the cost of drone courier service?


Far less than chartering airplanes and most likely less than automobile service. The exact cost per pickup depends on the number of sites in your network, how far apart they are, and the amount you’re moving.

How fast are drones?


About as fast as a car on the highway. The difference is that drones can fly in straight lines between your locations, and aren’t delayed by traffic. Plus, they fly the same speed to places where there is no road.
The low cost of drones allows you to make several pickups throughout the day, rather than holding all samples for a car at the end of the day.

Are drones safe?


Yes. Operating aircraft safely is a well-known process. Investment in high-quality equipment, skilled people, and prudent procedures have made air travel safer than any other means of transport.
We use the highest quality aircraft available and they’re operated by skilled people who maintain a high standard of training. Our team employs the same procedures used for passenger aircraft.
